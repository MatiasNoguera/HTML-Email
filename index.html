HTML EMAIL

HTML emails are email messages that are designed using HTML and CSS to create visually appealing and functional content. Unlike plain text emails, HTML emails can include images, links, text styles, and structured layout, allowing for more effective and engaging communication with recipients.

HTML emails allow for a visually appealing design with the ability to include images, colors, custom fonts, and complex layouts. HTML emails can include tracking tags that allow senders to monitor performance metrics such as open rates, click-through rates, and conversions.

Email Marketing

Email marketing involves transmitting promotional messages, usually to a collective audience, via email. In its widest interpretation, any email dispatched to a prospective or existing customer falls under the umbrella of email marketing.


Email marketing is a digital marketing strategy that involves the use of email to send commercial, promotional or informational messages to a specific audience. It is a powerful tool for businesses due to its ability to directly reach potential and existing customers in a personalized and direct manner.
